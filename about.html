ABOUT ME

From a young age, I have always been drawn to drawing and animation as my chosen medium, as I loved being taken into these movies and series and being able to understand and view the world through the lens of the people who created them. Since then, I have always known that is what I wanted to do. I hope to work on anything that I can get my hands on that can give people the same feelings I had when I interacted with those worlds, whether that be through big screen movies, TV series, or video games.
